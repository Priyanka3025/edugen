## Why this exists

Indian teachers spend 8–10 hours a week on prep work alone — building quizzes, writing lesson plans, marking papers. AI can handle most of that, freeing teachers for what they actually trained for: teaching.

EduGen Suite is built one tool at a time, in public, by someone who cares about Indian classrooms.
